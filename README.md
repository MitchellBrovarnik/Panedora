### Background Effects
The Settings page lets you choose from 10 animated background effects, all of which inherit the active theme's accent color:
*   **Waves**
*   **Orbs**
*   **Space**
*   **Grid**
*   **Particles**
*   **Rings**
*   **Reactive (Bars)**
*   **Reactive (Circle)**
*   **Reactive (Wave)**
*   **Static**

Your selected effect is saved and restored automatically on next launch.
