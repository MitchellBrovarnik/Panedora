Privacy & Security

Your credentials never leave your machine. Panedora takes a zero-trust, local-first approach to your data.

OS Keychain Encryption

Pandora credentials are stored using your operating system's native secure credential store, never in plain text.

No Third-Party Tracking

Zero analytics, telemetry, or external data collection. No cookies, no fingerprinting, no phone-home calls.

Local-Only Data

All session data, tokens, and preferences are stored locally on your device and wiped completely on sign-out.
