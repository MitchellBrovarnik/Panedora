Panedora
Features
Requires Pandora Premium or Plus

Your music deserves a better window.

Panedora is a premium desktop client for Pandora with immersive glassmorphism themes, live lyrics, audio visualizers, and a mini player that floats over everything.

Windows macOS Linux

Everything you need to listen better.

🎨

9 Glassmorphism Themes

Frosted glass panels, translucent islands, and full CSS variable theming that transforms every pixel instantly when you switch.

🎵

Live Synced Lyrics

Time-coded lyrics auto-scroll with the music, highlighted line by line. Choose from glow, pill, or full-line highlight styles.

🎧

Audio Visualizers

10 animated background effects powered by Web Audio API. Reactive bars, circles, and waves that move with your music.

💻

Always-on-Top Mini Player

A compact floating bar that stays above all windows, even borderless fullscreen games. Controls and feedback at a glance.

🎨

Adaptive Dynamic Theme

Samples your current album art and extracts a dominant color, repainting the entire UI in real time as songs change.

🔒

Private & Secure

Credentials encrypted with OS keychain. No third-party tracking. All data stored locally and wiped on sign-out.
